# Privacy Policy

**Attention Gate — Intentional Notification Control**

*Last updated: March 27, 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

Attention Gate ("we," "our," or "us") is built on a simple principle: you decide what deserves your attention.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Attention Gate mobile application (“the App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

If you do not agree, do not use the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Define

* **Notification Content:** Titles, body text, and schedules you create.
* **Image References:** URLs you provide for notification visuals.
* **Configuration Settings:** Preferences that control how and when notifications are delivered.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

* **Device Information:** Basic device data (model, OS version) required to deliver notifications reliably.
* **Usage Signals:** Limited, non-identifiable technical data (such as crash logs or performance metrics) used to improve stability and performance.
* **Push Tokens:** A device-specific token may be generated to enable notification delivery. This token is not linked to your identity and is used only for delivering notifications.

### 2.3 What We Do Not Collect

* No name, email, or phone number
* No location data
* No contacts, photos, or cross-app data
* No tracking technologies or behavioral profiling

Attention Gate is designed to operate without personal identity and does not require user accounts.

---

## 3. How Information Is Used

Information is used strictly to support the function of the App:

* Deliver notifications based on your defined intent
* Persist your configurations locally on your device
* Render images from URLs you provide
* Improve reliability through limited, non-identifiable technical data

No data is used for advertising or resale.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Control

### 4.1 Local-First Architecture

All notification data and settings are stored **locally on your device**.

We do not maintain remote databases of your content.

### 4.2 Security

Data remains on-device and is protected by the security model of your operating system. We do not transmit or store your content on external servers. However, no system is entirely immune to risk.

### 4.3 External Requests

If you include image URLs, your device will request those assets directly from external servers. Those servers operate independently of Attention Gate.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on infrastructure services such as:

* **Expo Notifications** (local notification delivery)
* **Expo Application Services** (app infrastructure)

These services may process device-level information (such as push tokens or technical diagnostics) solely to enable notification delivery and app stability.

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Attention Gate is not intended for users under 13.

We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 7. Your Control

### 7.1 Permissions

Notification permissions can be enabled or disabled at any time through your device settings.

### 7.2 Data Removal

All data is under your control and can be removed by:

* Deleting entries within the App
* Clearing app storage
* Uninstalling the App

### 7.3 Opt-Out

You can disable notifications without removing your configurations.

---

## 8. Data Retention

Data exists only on your device.

When the App is removed, the data is removed with it.

We do not retain user data externally.

---

## 9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. We may notify users of material changes through the App or app store updates. Continued use of the App reflects acceptance of any revisions.

---

## 10. International Use

Limited technical processing by third-party infrastructure providers may occur in jurisdictions where those services operate.

---

## 11. Regional Rights

### California (CCPA)

You have the right to know, request deletion, and understand how your data is handled.

We do not sell personal data and collect minimal information.

### European Economic Area (GDPR)

Processing is based on your consent and the necessity of providing core app functionality.

Because data is stored locally and not maintained on our servers, most rights can be exercised directly by you through your device.
